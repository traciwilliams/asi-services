Careers at ASI

After 38 years of providing leading, multi-cultural home care services in the Chicagoland area, we know without a doubt that what makes ASI successful is all about our caregivers. ASI cares for its caregivers with passion. In turn they pass on this caring and compassionate spirit to our clients. They are the very foundation of our ongoing success and what sets us apart from any other home care agency.

ASI is one of the few agencies that offers health and disability insurance benefits to their employees; as well as occupational health via Norwegian American Hospital Corporate Health Program. As such, we retain our caregivers longer than most agencies. Many of our caregivers have been with us 10 years or more and 94% of ASI clients give the agency an overall rating of Very Good or Excellent.

As a home care agency of choice, we are always on the lookout for compassionate, honest and intuitive caregivers to join our family of service providers. In many cases, people seeking employment at ASI are already passionate about providing service to others and may have been in a caregiving role in their personal lives.

Professional caregiving allows candidates to develop their innate caregiving skills into a fulfilling and rewarding career of helping others reach and maintain independent, dignified lives. Click below to find out more about our award winning training program and how you can get started on a rewarding career in home care.

As always, ASI is an Equal Opportunity Employer. We consider applicants for all positions without regard to race, color, religion, creed, gender, national origin, age, disability, marital or veteran status, or any other legally protected status.
