WHAT TO EXPECT working with ASI

At ASI we take a highly personalized approach to care for each of our clients. In order to generate personalized care plans, we begin each new relationship with a free phone consultation and a subsequent in-home assessment.

The initial in-home assessment focuses on determining the safety risks and challenges facing a client. Additionally, it's an opportunity for clients to ask questions and make specific requests relative to their care plan.

​ Personalized Care Plans are then presented by ASI and agreed upon by all parties before 'Matching.' Matching a client to a caregiver is based on personalities, personal preferences (likes and dislikes), primary language, skill requirements, certifications, etc.
